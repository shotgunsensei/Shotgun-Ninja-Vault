### Step 2: Log In to Tech Deck

1. Open the application URL (provided by your admin)
2. Click **"Log in with Replit"**
3. Authorize the application when prompted
4. You will be automatically added to the Xodus Technology Professionals workspace

That's it! No additional setup needed. Your role and permissions are pre-configured.

## Your Workspace

Once logged in, you'll have access to:

- **Dashboard** - Overview of activity and key stats
- **Clients** - Manage client records (you can bulk import from CSV)
- **Sites** - Track client locations and offices
- **Assets** - Inventory IT assets with serial numbers, IPs, etc.
- **Evidence** - Securely upload and manage digital evidence files
- **Audit Log** - View a complete trail of all actions

## Quick Tips

- **CSV Import**: On the Clients, Sites, and Assets pages, click "Import CSV" to bulk-import data. Download the template first, fill it in, then upload.
- **Evidence Upload**: Drag and drop files or click to upload. Files are automatically deduplicated using SHA-256 hashing.
- **Search**: Use the search bar on evidence pages to find files by name, tags, or content.
